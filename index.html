Vulture Mountains

One side of these mountains extremely dry and barren while the other is full of trees and wildlife.

It is common for people to perish when attempting to cross these mountains without proper supplies, or knowledge of how to safely navigate.

Vultures picking at the bones of past travelers is a common sight. They say when you enter these birds will circle above waiting for the moment you drop to the ground, dead or alive, they will feast.
